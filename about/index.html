About

I am passionate about connecting with individuals through the lens of the camera. As a people photographer, I focus on making women feel and look beautiful. I love being able create a transformation with individuals and capturing them through the lens of my camera.

I aim for a contemporary portrait style infused with fashion inspired poses. Included in all my sessions are hair and makeup by experienced makeup and hair artists. I look forward to a having a session with you. I'm not looking for models but rather I am looking to transform the everyday woman into a model for a day.
